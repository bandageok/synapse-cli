# Code of conduct

Synapse welcomes technical disagreement. Keep it about the work and make claims that other contributors can inspect or reproduce.

## Expected behavior

- Treat contributors and users with respect.
- Give specific feedback about code, documentation, or observed behavior.
- Make room for people with different experience levels and communication styles.
- Correct mistakes plainly and credit the people who found them.
- Protect private information, credentials, and vulnerability details.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or sexualized conduct
- Personal attacks or repeated disruption of technical discussion
- Publishing private information without permission
- Deliberately misleading reproduction steps, benchmarks, or security claims
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors through off-platform contact

## Enforcement

Report conduct problems to `bandageok@gmail.com`. Include links or screenshots when possible. The maintainer may edit or remove content, limit participation, or block accounts to protect the project and its contributors.

This policy applies to the repository, project Discussions, and project-run community spaces.
